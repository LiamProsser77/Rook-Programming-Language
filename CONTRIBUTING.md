Contributing to L#
Thanks for your interest in contributing to L#!

How to Contribute
Contributions are welcome in the form of:

Bug reports
Feature suggestions
Documentation improvements
General feedback
If you would like to contribute:

Open an issue to describe your idea or problem.
Be respectful and clear in your description.
The project maintainer will review suggestions as time allows.
